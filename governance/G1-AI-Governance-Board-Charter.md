## 1. Purpose

The AI Governance Board provides oversight, review, and decision-making authority for all AI initiatives under the DIVE Framework. The Board ensures alignment with organizational strategy, ethical standards, regulatory requirements, and risk appetite.

---

## 2. Composition

| Role | Appointed By | Term |
|------|-------------|------|
| Chair (senior executive) | CEO | 2 years |
| Chief Data/AI Officer | CEO | Standing |
| Ethics Officer | Board | 2 years |
| Legal/Compliance Representative | General Counsel | 2 years |
| Head of Risk | CRO | 2 years |
| Independent Subject Matter Expert | Chair | 1 year (renewable) |

**Quorum:** 4 of 6 members, including Chair or Vice-Chair.

---

## 3. Authority

The Board has exclusive authority to:

| Authority | Description |
|-----------|------------|
| **Project Approval** | Approve or reject AI project proposals at the Diagnose gate |
| **Gate Decisions** | Make Go / Conditional Go / Redesign / Kill decisions at phase gates |
| **Ethics Oversight** | Require additional ethics reviews beyond standard screening |
| **Decommissioning** | Mandate model decommissioning for cause |
| **Exception Handling** | Approve exceptions to governance policy |
| **Budget Allocation** | Approve organizational compute budget allocations |

---

## 4. Meeting Cadence

| Meeting Type | Frequency | Participants |
|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| Project review | Bi-weekly | PM + Tech Lead (by invite) |
| Full board | Monthly | All members |
| Emergency session | As needed | Chair convenes |

---

## 5. Decision-Making Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Standard decisions | Majority vote |
| Tie-breaking | Chair has deciding vote |
| Kill decisions | Supermajority required (2/3 of members) |
| Emergency decisions | Chair + 1 member; ratified at next full board |
| Recusal | Members must recuse if conflict of interest exists |

---

## 6. Reporting Structure

The Board reports to:
- **Executive Committee** — quarterly summary
- **Board of Directors** — annual AI risk report
- **Regulatory bodies** — as required by applicable law

---

## 7. Conflicts of Interest

Board members must disclose any direct personal or financial interest in projects under review. Recusal is mandatory. The disclosure log is maintained by the Board Secretary.

---

## 8. Charter Amendments

This charter may be amended by a 2/3 supermajority vote of the Board. Amendments must be documented with version history.
